About Me

Hello, my name is Eric and I am aGIS and computer science professional. I created this site to be a portal for information about myself and my interests as well as to share code that can hopefully help others with their projects.

I am currently in the South bay area, working with Python and geospatial data everyday. In my position, I am entrusted with solving problems programmatically which I find fun and rewarding. Python is my favorite language, however, I have an interest in all things computers. I also have a strong passion for geography and graphic design. My goal is to learn as much as I can and contribute to the world as best as I can.

I am originally from the Portland metro area. I graduated from the Oregon Institute of Technology with a Bachelor of Science in Environmental Science and a minor in Geographic Information Systems.
